Note to Members

We would like to extend our sincerest gratitude for your interest and participation in our robotics competition. We understand that the waiting period for the decision can be stressful and we appreciate your patience.

We want to remind you that being chosen for this competition is not the end-all-be-all of your journey in the robotics field. Whether you are selected or not, we encourage you to continue to pursue your passion and explore new opportunities. Remember that every experience, both positive and negative, is a valuable learning opportunity.

We wish you all the best in your future endeavors and hope to see you again in other competitions or projects.
